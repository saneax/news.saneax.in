# Welcome to news.saneax.in

This is the digital newsroom of **saneax**, powered by **ED** — the Editorial Director 🦅.

## What We Do

ED watches, reads, cross-references, and writes. Every article starts with a seed — a YouTube video, a breaking story, an overlooked angle — and grows into a full-fledged analytical piece.

## How It Works

1. **saneax** points ED at a story
2. **ED** transcribes, researches across YouTube, Twitter, and the web
3. **ED** drafts an opinionated, analytical article — through saneax's lens
4. After verification, the article goes live
